GTA is led by an experienced CEO who successfully established manufacturing facilities in Asia and product distribution throughout Western Europe. We offer our clients an extensive network of relationships in China, Taiwan, Hong Kong, South Korea, Indonesia, Vietnam, The United Kingdom, France and Germany. ’

Based on years of practical experience, our firm provides specialized advice for companies seeking to ‘offshore’ manufacturing. With multiple relationships throughout Asia and utilizing respected sourcing companies and supply chain logistics experts, it allows us to offer our clients a turn-key service that permits rapid deployment of their offshore strategy at a minimal cost and risk. We also provide advice on international marketing and distribution strategies, as well as customs and duty matters and supply chain management issues. GTA is closely aligned with Ater Wynne LLP , which offers specialized counsel on the formation of offshore entities and joint ventures and wholly-owned foreign enterprises as well as on international tax matters. Our practical business experience, combined with broad commercial transaction expertise, positions GTA as trusted advisors in the global economy.

Fees

GTA charges its clients a modest monthly retainer for the services it provides. This retainer does not include any legal services that may be provided by Ater Wynne, LLP , our alliance partner for international legal services.
